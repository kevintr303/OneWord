About OneWord

OneWord is a dynamic platform where users can submit a single word and vote on the best ones.

  • You can submit one word every 5 minutes.
  • Vote up or down to adjust the ranking.
  • Words remain visible for 24 hours.
  • Submitting a duplicate word counts as a vote.
